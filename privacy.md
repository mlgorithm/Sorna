# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Sorna is designed to minimize data collection.

## Data collection

Sorna does not require an account.

Sorna does not sell personal information.

Sorna does not use advertising identifiers.

Sorna does not track users across apps or websites.

## Data stored on device

Sorna may store user preferences such as followed topics, reading queue, saved items, and app settings locally on your device.

## Network requests

Sorna fetches public research metadata and content from third-party sources such as arXiv, OpenAlex, and research RSS feeds to display papers inside the app.

## Third-party links

When you open a paper PDF, publisher page, or other external link, you leave the app and are subject to the third party's privacy policy.

## Children's privacy

Sorna is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue at [github.com/mlgorithm/Sorna/issues](https://github.com/mlgorithm/Sorna/issues).
